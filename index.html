Additional Services

In addition to our core recruiting services, Talentish HR offers a range of HR services to help businesses manage their HR needs. Here are a few additional services we offer:

  1. Employee engagement and retention: We help businesses develop strategies to engage and retain their employees. Our services include employee surveys, focus groups, and training programs that are designed to improve employee satisfaction, reduce turnover, and boost productivity.
  2. HR compliance: We help businesses stay compliant with federal, state, and local employment laws and regulations. Our services include policy development, audit and compliance reviews, and training programs for HR staff and managers.
  3. Performance management: We offer performance management solutions that help businesses set goals, track progress, and provide feedback to employees. Our services include performance appraisals, goal-setting, and coaching and development programs.
  4. Leadership development: We provide training and coaching programs to help businesses develop their leaders and managers. Our services include leadership assessments, executive coaching, and management development programs.
  5. HR outsourcing: We offer full or partial outsourcing solutions that enable businesses to outsource their HR functions to our team of experienced professionals. Our services include HR administration, payroll processing, benefits management, and compliance.

We understand that every business has unique HR needs, which is why we offer customized solutions tailored to meet those needs. Whether you're a small start-up or a large corporation, we have the expertise and experience to help you manage your HR needs effectively.
